Hello! I'm Chu-Yu, a first-year master student at UMSI.

ACADEMIA

My focus is User Experience Research and Design and I love to talk to people to know more stories and understand their motivations. Based on the psychological background, I believe the core of design is to feel empathy with users.

As a designer, I am open to the differences in the world and willing to embrace all the possibilities. I am passionate to experience as much as possible, where I am inspired and gain more insights.

EXTRACURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

I participated in orchestra and played the flute in senior high. From then on, music accompanies me all the time that I can always find inspirations and pluck up the courage to overcome any obstacles when surrounded by music.

Also, after came to the US, I tried lots of activities which I haven't experienced in my home country. I went to ice-skating and snowboarding with friends which did broaden my vision in a completely different yet fresh way.

EVERY DOT CONNECTS

I believe these various experiences make great impact on my thoughts as well as my design unconsciously. Only when I am an open-minded designer can I come up with innovative ideas!

I am also an active learner that pays attention to new technology and design trends. I listen to the podcast at night to keep myself up-to-date. I love to discuss dissimilar opinions with my friends which allows us to take different perspectives into consideration in order to increase our creativity.

As mentioned before, I love music as well sound a lot. I'm trying to connect user experience with music and sound; therefore, I'm exploring Voice User Interfaces and Music Technology currently.
